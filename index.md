## About

Perquackey is a frantic word dice game for iPhone and iPad, rebuilt from the 1956 tabletop classic. Roll ten dice, race the three-minute clock, and build as many words as you can before time runs out. Filling all five slots in a word-length group unlocks stacking consecutive-length bonuses. Hit 2,000 points and the stakes go up — the vulnerability mechanic adds red dice to your pool and penalizes under-500 turns.

Three modes: Classic (1–4 players, pass-and-play), Daily Challenge (one shared puzzle per day with streak tracking), and Unlimited (no timer, no pressure). Full Game Center integration, per-game word analysis with definitions, and lifetime statistics.

Daily Challenge and Unlimited Mode are free forever, complete with post-game word analysis (including definitions) and full statistics. Classic Mode (1–4 player pass-and-play, turn-based, first to 5,000) is unlocked with a single $2.99 in-app purchase — no ads, no subscriptions, no in-app currency.
